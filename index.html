FAQ

Common questions

Do my clients know it's built on TES? +
No. Your clients only see your brand — your logo, your name, your colours. TES is completely invisible. You can disable the "Powered by TES" attribution in White Label settings.
What does "zero processing fees" mean? +
TES CRM never takes a cut of your client payments. You set up your own Stripe account and receive 100% of what your clients pay. We only charge the flat monthly platform fee.
How do the automation workflows work? +
You choose a trigger (e.g. "new client joins") and an action (e.g. "send welcome email"). TES CRM runs the automation automatically every time the trigger fires. No coding required — just pick from our pre-built templates or build your own in 4 steps.
Can clients book sessions directly? +
Yes. You get a shareable booking link (e.g. book.yourbusiness.com). Clients choose a service, pick a time from your available slots, and confirm. You see it instantly in your calendar and they receive an automated confirmation.
Can I charge clients on any billing cycle? +
Yes — daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, every 6 months, or one-time. You can mix and match per client or per plan.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with access to every feature from day one. No credit card required to start.
